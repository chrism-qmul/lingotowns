Game Instructions

Welcome to LingoTowns! This is the map of the world. You can explore different towns and find games in each building. By clicking the town icon you will begin playing one of the minigames. There are three minigames. The farm where you can start harvesting crops for the town by selecting the correct noun phrases The bakery where you can bake goods for the town by labeling part of speech in a text The library where you can help the town understand archived documents by filling in the blank and completing word puzzles

That's it for now! Enjoy exploring LingoTowns and help our people understand your language.
